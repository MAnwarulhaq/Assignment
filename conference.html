International Conference on Artificial Intelligence

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence (ICAI), to be held by National Center of Artificial Intelligence (NCAI) at the premises of National University of Sciences and Technologies in Islamabad. This will be a 2-day conference bringing together an international community of practitioners and researchers to meet and share cutting-edge developments in the field.
